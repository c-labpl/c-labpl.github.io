Cognitive and Neural Architecture of Consciousness

Peyia, Cyprus, 20-24 of March, 2019

We kindly invite You to participate in our 10th Scientific Meeting of the Consciousness Lab.

This time we are making it even more unique by visiting Cyprus, the place where the C-lab story has begun. The 2019 meeting will be special not only as it will mark the 10th anniversary of the Lab but also it will allow us to start a new decade by discussing our new projects investigating Cognitive and Neural Architecture of Consciousness to be realised within the Pan-European network Skuldnet we have recently joined.

We invite to participate all Consciousness Lab members, affiliates, collaborators as well as all researchers interested in the topic.
