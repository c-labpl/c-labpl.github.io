Aspects of Consciousness Research

Kościelisko, 27 of February to 02 of March, 2020

We kindly invite You to participate in our 10th Scientific Meeting of the Consciousness Lab.

The 2020 meeting will allow us to talk about our collaborative research projects investigating cognitive and neural characteristics of consciousness, carried out i.a. within the COST grant and the Pan-European network Skuldnet. During the meeting, we will discuss consciousness-related theories and hypotheses as well as methodology and practical execution of research. The additional goal of the meeting is to expand scientific insight into aspects of consciousness research by adapting interdisciplinary (philosophical, psychological, neuroscientific) perspective.

We invite to participate all Consciousness Lab members, affiliates, collaborators as well as all researchers interested in the topic.
